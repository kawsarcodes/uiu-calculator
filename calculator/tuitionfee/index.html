Fee Calculation & Payment Policies

Basic Fee Formula

$$ \text{Gross Fee} = (\text{Total Credits} \times \text{Per Credit Rate}) + \text{Trimester Fee} $$

Fee Components & Rules:

  1. Tuition Fee: Calculated based on total credit hours registered.
  2. Trimester Fee: Fixed charge of 6,500à§³ per term.
  3. Late Fee: 500à§³ if registration is after deadline.

Retake Fee Policy

Waiver eligibility for repeating a course:

  • 1st Retake: 50% waiver on tuition fee.
  • 2nd & Subsequent Retakes: Full tuition fee required.

Note: Trimester Fee (6,500à§³) is always mandatory.

$$ \text{Cost} = \begin{cases} 50\% \text{ Tuition}, & \text{if 1st attempt} \\ 100\% \text{ Tuition}, & \text{if 2nd+ attempt} \end{cases} $$

Waiver Eligibility

Guidelines: Waivers apply only to tuition of new credits.

  1. Higher value between General Waiver and Merit Scholarship is applied.
  2. Other waivers are added on top.
$$ \text{Payable} = \text{Gross} - \min(\text{Waiver}, \text{New Tuition}) $$

Installment Breakdown

Standard Rule (40-30-30):

  1. 1st installment calculated on Gross Fee.
  2. Waivers adjusted in 2nd and 3rd installments.
$$ \text{1st Inst.} = \text{Gross Fee} \times 40\% $$
Practical Example: If Gross Fee is 65k but Net Payable after waiver is 6.5k, you only pay 6.5k. All dues must be cleared to unlock next registration.
