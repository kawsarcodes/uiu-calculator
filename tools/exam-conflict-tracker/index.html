What is this tool & How to Use

What is this tool?

The Exam Conflict Tracker is specifically designed for students planning to take extra courses or retakes. It helps you simulate your trimester load to ensure your exam dates don't clash before you finalize your advising.

Why use it?
(01) Avoid Clashes: It instantly detects if two courses have exams at the exact same time.
(02) Manage Pressure: It warns you if you have multiple exams on the same day.
(03) Visual Planning: It maps your courses onto a visual timeline so you can see your exam gaps clearly.

How to Use

(01) Select all the courses you plan to take next trimester.
(02) Check the boxes below for any Red or Yellow alerts.

Understanding Alerts

Critical Conflict (Red):
Two exams clash at the exact same time. You cannot take these courses together.

Pressure Warning (Yellow):
Multiple exams are on the same day (different times). This is allowed, but stressful.
