Tracking the evolution of the UIU Calculator platform.

20 Feb, 2026

Persistence & UX

v2.4

Focus on data retention and user experience improvements for long-term planning.

  • NewAuto Save (User Input): Inputs like Completed Credits, Current CGPA, Credit Fee, Trimester Fee, etc. are now saved even after refreshing the page.
  • ImpEdit Modals: Introduced popup modals for editing courses and retakes without needing to delete them first.
  • FixResolved an issue where retake courses in the Planner were not saving correctly.
19 Feb, 2026

Visual Analytics

v2.3

Integration of Chart.js to provide visual feedback on academic progress.

  • NewMulti-Sem Chart: Line graph showing required GPA vs. Current GPA trajectory over future trimesters.
  • NewGraduation Projection: Visual path showing if the target CGPA is achievable by graduation.
  • NewWhat-If Graph: Scenario based chart showing how different GPAs affect final results.
18 Feb, 2026

Advanced Simulation

v2.2

Added "What-If" analysis and detailed retake impact logic.

  • NewWhat-If Calculator: Simulate custom scenarios (e.g., "What happens if I get 3.5 in 12 credits?").
  • LogicRetake Impact Analysis: Detailed breakdown showing exact CGPA boost from retaking specific courses.
  • UXAdded color-coded alerts (Green/Red) to indicate if a retake actually improves the CGPA or not.
14 Feb, 2026

Tuition Fee Overhaul

v2.1

Complete rewrite of the Tuition Fee Calculator to match UIU's complex policies.

  • NewWaiver Support: Added inputs for Sibling, Ethnic, and Disability waivers.
  • LogicInstallment Breakdown: Added logic for "40% in 1st Installment" vs "Waiver in 1st Installment".
  • FixFixed scholarship calculation error on retake courses.
12 Feb, 2026

Target Planner Release

v2.0

Major update introducing the Reverse CGPA Calculator.

  • MajorTarget Planner Module: Calculate required GPA to reach a specific target CGPA.
  • NewGraduation Mode: Calculate required GPA for remaining credits to graduate with honors.
  • NewStrategy Engine: Suggests grade combinations to achieve targets.
